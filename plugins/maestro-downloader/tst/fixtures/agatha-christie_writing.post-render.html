    What you'll learn

    Agatha Christie’s own words have been used to create this groundbreaking course, so that you can finally learn her writing secrets and plot out your very own murder mystery.

    • Structure an airtight plot
    • Create compelling characters
    • Master twists, turns and red herrings
    • Course crafted by Christie scholars
    • Made in collaboration with her family
    • Study how Agatha wrote

    Your Maestro

    Christie didn't just write crime fiction; she defined the genre. With unforgettable characters like Miss Marple and Hercule Poirot, 66 novels published, over 2 billion books sold, and the world's longest-running play – The Mousetrap – her influence is unmatched.

    Now you can learn from the woman herself. She shares the mechanics of a good mystery, like placing clues, developing characters, and writing the perfect reveal. With these and the secrets to Christie's timeless warmth and humanity at your fingertips, you could create the world's next lovable sleuth.
