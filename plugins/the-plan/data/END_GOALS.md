## EG-1: Increase the number of awakened beings on the planet

`id: eg1-awakening`

**Working definition:**
A being is "more awakened" to the extent that they directly perceive the nature of experience with reduced reactivity, increased equanimity, and increased compassionate responsiveness. A being is "fully awakened" after they have had a direct experience of the truth of the nature of the Self, i.e. is no longer 100% of the time automatically identifying with the 'story of who I am' - perceiving who they are without falling into the dual mental construct of the ego identify. A being is "enlightened" if they don't identify with the ego at substantially all of the time; though ego appearances may still occur the being is not taken-in by them and is able to recognize and auto-correct being taken-in by them more or less immediately, most of the time. A being is "fully enlightened" if there is no remainder, i.e. they never are taken-in by them. This is not a religious or sectarian claim — many traditions and many secular practices produce movement along this dimension. "Increase the number of awakened beings on the planet" == maximize the number of people moving towards fully enlightened and how far each one is on the path from more awakened -> fully awakened -> enlightened -> fully enlightened.

## EG-2: Address climate change

`id: eg2-climate`

**Working definition:**
Reduce greenhouse gas concentrations and/or increase planetary resilience to climate disruption already in motion.

## EG-3: Support democratic institutions and constrain bad governance

`id: eg3-governance`

**Working definition (reframed from "reduce bad governments and fascism"):**
Support institutions that constrain authoritarian power: journalism, human rights monitoring, dissident protection, civic literacy.
Reduce the number of people living under regimes that systematically violate human rights or suppress dissent.
Minimize or eliminate the presence and power of non-benevolent authoritarians, dictators, and fascists.
De-ossify institutions that have come to be an obstacle to healthy governance.
Promote critical thinking and media analysis.

## EG-4: Reduce needless suffering, broadly

`id: eg4-reduce-suffering`

**Working definition:**
Suffering that arises from causes we know how to address — poverty, untreated illness, isolation, despair, factory-farm conditions, psychologically caused suffering (unskilled beliefs), physiologically caused suffering, etc. for sentient beings.

## Cross-cutting commitments

- Means matter as much as ends. See `ETHICS.md`.
- Local and immediate actions do count. Helping one person well is not a distraction from helping a billion poorly. But keep it in proportion: if a $1000 investment helps one person eat for a month vs. $1000 investment buys malaria treatment for everyone in Ghana, we would prefer the latter.
- Build leverage early; act broadly later. See `ENABLERS/`.
- Stay willing to abandon a wedge that isn't working. See REVIEWS cadence.
