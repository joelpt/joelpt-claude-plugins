- Q-002: For EG-1, do we have access to or can we build evaluation criteria that distinguish real benefit from felt benefit?
- Q-003: For EG-2 (climate), is direct software/AI work plausible at our scale, or is the realistic contribution dollars routed to high-leverage orgs?
- Q-004: For EG-3 (governance), is this end goal even appropriate to pursue actively given the ethics constraint against political influence, or should it be reframed as "support institutions that constrain bad governments" (journalism, human rights monitoring, dissident tooling)?
- Q-005: What is the right balance between "give effectively now" (GiveWell-style) and "build capacity to give much more later" (capacity building) at our current resource level?
